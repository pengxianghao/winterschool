Faculty Members

Wei Song
Associate Professor, YMSC, Tsinghua University

Research interests:
String theory, quantum gravity and holography. In particular, I work on holographic dualities for spacetimes which are not asymptotically Anti-de Sitter, including the Kerr/CFT correspondence, and flat holography. My recent work inludes holographic entanglement entropy for non-AdS holography, the Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev models and integrable irrelevant deformations.

Bartek Czech
Member, Institute for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University

Research interests:
I study the emergence of spacetime and gravity from quantum information theory, especially in the context of the AdS/CFT correspondence. I am interested in information theoretic and operational interpretations of bulk concepts (such as differential entropy or bit threads), the uses of tensor networks for understanding spacetime, constraints on states with semiclassical bulk duals, circuit complexity conjectures and similar topics. Most recently, I have been pursuing the idea that modular Berry phases (phases arising from varying modular Hamiltonians) in the CFT are responsible for the curvature (and certain other aspects) of the AdS geometry.
